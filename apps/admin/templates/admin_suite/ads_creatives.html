{# ═══ How Creatives Work ═══ #}

Creative = the actual ad asset (banner image, native card, HTML embed, or script tag).

Types: Banner = image ad with click URL. Native = blended content card. HTML = custom HTML embed. Script = JS ad tag (AdSense etc).

Campaign Link: Every creative belongs to a campaign. The campaign controls budget, scheduling, and caps.

Weight: Higher weight = shown more often in rotation. Weight 2 shows 2x more than weight 1.

Assign to Placement: Click the link icon to map a creative to a placement slot. One creative can be assigned to multiple placements.

Toggle: On/Off controls whether this creative is active. Inactive creatives are not served.

Workflow: Campaign exists → Create Creative for that campaign → Assign to placement slots → Active creative rotates in those slots.
